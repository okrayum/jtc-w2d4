I enjoy exploring newly opened eateries as well as hidden gems that have been around awhile. My tastes are pretty diverse, but I especially enjoy Asian, Mexican, and various fusion cuisines.

Here are a few places I visited recently:

  • Brothers Family Restaurant:A spacious diner with a vast menu of American comfort food, including meatloaf & breakfast faves. As well as Italian, Greek, and Mexican inspired dishes. (Website)
  • Scorpion Bay Grill: A day of fun on the water is bound to make you hungry—not to mention thirsty. When it’s time to refuel, our floating restaurant offers a casual respite with a full menu of fresh salads, sandwiches, burgers, and cocktails, all served up with 360-degree mountain views. (Website)
  • Barrio Brewing Co.:Arizona’s oldest brewery, opened back in 1991 first as Gentle Bens in Tucson, AZ by founders Dennis & Tauna Arnold. They had a love for great food, amazing craft beer and an aspiration to change the landscape of Arizona brewing.. (Website)
